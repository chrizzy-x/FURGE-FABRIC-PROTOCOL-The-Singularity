# Demo Guide

## Flagship Scenario

1. Submit a medical protocol query from `medical-demo`.
2. Resolve supporting evidence through `ResearchChain`.
3. Produce cost and risk analysis through `FinanceChain`.
4. Execute a deterministic mock bridge action with consensus validation.
5. Rent or transfer a certified skill in the marketplace.
6. Record a metaverse presence update and a watch-to-takeover handoff.
7. Confirm the full audit trace in `explorer`.

## Domain Demos

- `medical-demo`: diagnosis and treatment-plan consensus
- `finance-demo`: market and risk synthesis
- `research-demo`: literature and trial evidence synthesis
- `legal-demo`: clause and compliance review
- `education-demo`: participation and progress validation
- `metaverse-demo`: presence, session review, and control handoff
